Business FAQ Chatbot Demo

This is a demonstration of the Business FAQ Chatbot widget. Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to start a conversation.

How to Implement on Your Website

To add this chatbot to your website, include the following code:

<!-- Include the widget script -->
<script src="path/to/widget.js"></script>

<!-- Initialize the widget -->
<script>
  document.addEventListener('DOMContentLoaded', function() {
    new BusinessChatWidget({
      apiKey: 'your-api-key-here',
      apiEndpoint: 'https://your-lambda-api-endpoint.amazonaws.com/prod/chat',
      widgetTitle: 'Business Assistant',
      primaryColor: '#4a90e2'
    });
  });
</script>

Customize the configuration options to match your business needs.
